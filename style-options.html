Three visual directions to explore

Here are three distinct style concepts you can evaluate before committing to a redesign.

Clean, premium, and highly readable.

+
Technology & Platform Leader

Story-driven, personal, and content first.

Option C — High-Tech Dark

Bold, energetic, and product-minded.

+
+ + + +
+
+
Platform outcomes at enterprise scale
+
+
+
+
    +
  • Retains your current dark-mode DNA with cleaner restraint.

Suggested direction

If you want a strong professional signal while still feeling current, start from Option A and blend in selected accents from Option C.
